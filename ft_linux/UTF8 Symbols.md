**Hats and accents (for estimators like ŷ)**  
There's no single "hat" codepoint for arbitrary letters — instead you combine a letter with a combining circumflex U+0302. So ŷ = y + U+0302. Some letters have precomposed versions:

- ŷ — U+0177 (precomposed, works in most fonts)
- x̂ — x + U+0302 (combining, since there's no precomposed x-hat)
- θ̂ — θ + U+0302
